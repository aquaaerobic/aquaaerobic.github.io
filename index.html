How do I begin beta testing app "AquaGuide: 1-Minute Reference Guide"?

  • Open Safari web browser from iPhone or iPad
  • Go to http://udid.io
  • Tap the "Tap to find UDID"
  • Tap "Allow", if prompted
  • Tap Install
  • Input your Passcode, if prompted
  • Tap Install
  • Select choice to email and send UDID to ckonkol@aqua-aerobic.com
  • Confirmation email from ckonkol@aqua-aerobic.com will arrive within 24 hours
  • Once Confirmation email arrives go to DOWNLOAD AND INSTALL LATEST VERSION

DOWNLOAD AND INSTALL LATEST VERSION!

  • Click HERE from your iPhone or iPad
  • Continue on to below Trust App

TRUST APP

  • When you first open the app you see a notification that the developer of the app isn't trusted on your device. You can dismiss this message.
  • After you dismiss this message, you can establish trust for the app developer. Tap Settings > General > Device Management. Tap "Aqua-Aerobic Systems, Inc."
  • Tap Trust "Aqua-Aerobic Systems, Inc" and accept trust prompt to verify that you trust the app
  • You are now ready to use the app
