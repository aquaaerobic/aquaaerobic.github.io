ANDROID USERS:

DOWNLOAD AND INSTALL LATEST VERSION!

  1. From an Android Phone, go to Settings > Security (in title) > and check "Unknown Sources" to allow your phone to install apps from sources other than the Google Play Store.
  2. Make sure you have fast internet
  3. Tap here to install app and accept all prompts (if any is given)

iPHONE USERS:

DOWNLOAD AND INSTALL LATEST VERSION!

  1. Tap & hold existing "AquaGuide" app & select "Delete App"
  2. Tap HERE from your iPhone & when prompted tap Install



  3. If this is your first time installing this app (or you are reinstalling app), follow steps below Trust App section
  4. After a few moments, find & tap on app icon AquaGuide
  5. That's it! Your should now have the latest updates!

TRUST APP

  1. Tap Settings > General > Device Management > Aqua-Aerobic Systems, Inc.



  2. Tap Trust "Aqua-Aerobic Systems, Inc"



  3. Tap Trust prompt to verify that you trust the app



  4. You are now ready to use the app



  5. Open app

  6. Enjoy!
